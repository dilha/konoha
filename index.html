Konoha - Shinobi village

Konoha (木ノ葉隠れの里, Konohagakure no Sato, literally means Village Hidden In Leaves or Hidden Village Foliage) is a hidden Shinobi village in the Land of Fire and one of the five main hidden Shinobi villages.

Hokages:

  • The First Hokage Hashirama Senju (千手 柱 間)
  • The Second Hokage Tobirama Senju (千手 扉 間)
  • The Third Hokage Hiruzen Sarutobi (猿 飛 ヒ ル ゼ ン)
  • The Fourth Hokage Minato Namikaze (波風ミナト)
  • The Fifth Hokage Tsunade (綱手)
  • The Sixth Hokage Kakashi Hatake (はたけ カカシ)
  • The Seventh Hokage Naruto Uzumaki (うずまきナルト)

Konoha is a place where I dream to visit.
